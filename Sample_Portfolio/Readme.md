## Getting started

All documents in this portfolio are written for a fictional product called **WorkIt**.

### About WorkIt

WorkIt is a cloud-based case management product that helps support teams create, assign, track, and resolve cases from a single interface. Teams use WorkIt to manage customer issues, internal requests, and operational workflows.

WorkIt has three core resources:

- **Cases** — individual issues or requests that need to be resolved
- **Projects** — groups of related cases organized by team or goal
- **Users** — team members who are assigned and resolve cases

WorkIt is not a real product. It exists solely as a practice subject for this portfolio. All API endpoints, responses, and product details are fictional.
